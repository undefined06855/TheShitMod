# The Shit Mod

A ROUNDS mod for me and my friends it's great and fire and amazing.

You'll never build it yourself, but if you want to then make sure to set the paths in TheShitMod.csproj to ones that
work for you.

## Cards
- Damager (Common): Modifies your damage by 0.5x to 1.5x every round. Per. Round.
- fuuck im falling over hold on (Common): Tilts the screen of the person you shoot by 10 degrees, resets at the start of rounds.
- The Green (Common): Greenifies your eyes (and gives you health and regeneration and lifesteal)
- Growth Ray (Legendary): YEAAAH GROWTH RAY YOU CAN GROW THINGS WHEN YOU SHOOT THEM (for 2s)
- Shrink Ray (Legendary): YEAAAH SHRINK RAY YOU CAN SHRINK THINGS WHEN YOU SHOOT THEM (for 2s)
- Slime (Rare): Makes your bullets really slimy and bouncy. Like, really slimy.
- SPEAK TO THE HAND (Rare): Multiplies health by 0.0000000001x but gives you 0.01x block cooldown. I heard you liked blocking!
- Swap Hands (Common): Swaps your position with the person you shoot (like Uno, that card that nobody follows?)
- The 1% (Common): Adds 1% to every stat, positive or negative. Uh, doesn't really do much.
- This Is One Big Magazine (Rare): Gives you 35 ammo, but makes your reload time 20 seconds. Hope you can use those 35 bullets well!
- Washing Machine License (Rare): Puts whoever you shoot into a washing machine and spins them around. Sog
